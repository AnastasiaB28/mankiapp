Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Manki ("the App") is a flashcard application for iOS developed by Anastasiia Bosakevych ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Manki is a fully offline, local-first application. All data you create — including decks, cards, and study progress — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or share any of your data with any third party. Your data remains entirely under your control.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies, pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

5. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or disclose any personal information to third parties.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

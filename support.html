Frequently Asked Questions

How does Manki work?
Each card shows a sentence with a missing word. Tap to reveal the answer with an explanation. Swipe right if you caught it, swipe left if you missed it. Manki tracks which words you've learned so you can focus on the ones you haven't.
Is Manki free?
Yes! All B1-B2 intermediate decks (6 topics, 300 cards) are completely free. Advanced C1-C2 content is available with Manki Premium.
What is Manki Premium?
Manki Premium unlocks all 6 advanced C1-C2 decks with 300 additional vocabulary cards. You can start with a 7-day free trial, subscribe monthly, or purchase lifetime access with a one-time payment.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Manki and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I restore my purchases?
Open Manki, tap on any locked deck, and select "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Does Manki work offline?
Yes. All your decks and progress are stored locally on your device. No internet connection is needed to study.
Will my progress transfer to a new device?
Currently Manki stores data locally on your device. Cloud sync is not available yet. Purchases can be restored on a new device via "Restore Purchases."

Manage Your Subscription

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Manki to manage or cancel
